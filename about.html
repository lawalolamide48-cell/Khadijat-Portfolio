About me

Organized by experience.
Curious by nature.

Khadijat Olamide Lawal

Administrative Professional & Full-Stack Developer

I am an administrative professional and full-stack developer with a practical understanding of how people, information, and processes work together.

My background spans executive support, office operations, customer service, financial reporting, scheduling, and digital communication. Through Tech Studio Academy's Full-Stack programme I built on that foundation with a complete web development toolkit: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, TypeScript, Node.js, Express, REST APIs, MongoDB, Tailwind CSS, Vite, and Git — covering everything from responsive interfaces to secured backend services and cloud deployment.

Over four years of administrative experience, I developed a keen eye for efficiency, accuracy, and stakeholder communication. I have managed executive calendars, prepared briefing documents, tracked departmental budgets, processed invoices, and coordinated complex schedules across multiple organisations — all while maintaining a high standard of professionalism in both on-site and fully remote environments.

Transitioning into technology felt like a natural extension of that operational mindset. I approach software with the same attention to detail I brought to office management: every interface decision has a human on the other end, and every line of code either helps or hinders their experience.

That combination lets me approach software with empathy and structure. I care about interfaces that feel clear, responsible, and genuinely useful — and about the operational thinking that makes digital products work in the real world.

I am currently open to frontend development, full-stack, administrative, executive support, and hybrid operations roles — on-site or remote.
